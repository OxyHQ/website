Creating your Oxy ID is a one-time event. Take five minutes to do it carefully — the choices you make here are the foundation of everything you'll do on Oxy.

## Before you start

Have these ready:

- A device with a screen (phone, tablet, or computer)
- A way to write down 24 words securely (paper, a password manager, a metal backup plate)
- A recovery email (we'll add this in step 3 — optional but recommended)

## Walkthrough

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Accounts">
    Visit [accounts.oxy.so](https://accounts.oxy.so) or open the Accounts app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a username">
    Your username is permanent and visible. It'll be how people @ you in Mention and address you in Inbox. Choose carefully.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate your identity">
    Tap **Create identity**. Your device generates a fresh keypair — this happens locally, the private key never leaves the device until you back it up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write down your recovery phrase">
    24 words appear. Write them down, in order, on paper. Do not screenshot. Do not store digitally. This phrase is the only way to recover your account if you lose your device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the phrase">
    Accounts asks you to re-enter a few of the words in order, to make sure you actually wrote them down. If you can't, **stop**, write them again, and try the confirmation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a recovery email (optional)">
    A recovery email is a convenience layer — see [Add a recovery email](/help/account/add-recovery-email) for what it does and doesn't protect against.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you have now

Your Oxy ID is live. Every Oxy app — Inbox, Mention, Console — will sign you in automatically when you open it.

<Callout type="danger" title="Test your recovery phrase">
  Before you put anything important into your account, do one full recovery test on a spare device. The phrase only works if it's correct.
</Callout>

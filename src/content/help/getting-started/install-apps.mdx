The Oxy apps are available on iOS, Android, macOS, Windows, Linux, and the web. You only need to install the ones you use.

## Download links

- **Accounts** — manage your Oxy ID, devices, and security settings. Available on every platform.
- **Inbox** — end-to-end encrypted email. iOS, Android, web.
- **Console** — developer dashboard for building on top of Oxy. Web only.

<Callout type="tip" title="Don't want to install anything?">
  Every Oxy app has a full web version. Visit [accounts.oxy.so](https://accounts.oxy.so) or [inbox.oxy.so](https://inbox.oxy.so) and sign in.
</Callout>

## Sign in across devices

Sign in with the same Oxy ID on every device. Your sessions sync automatically and you can revoke any device from Accounts → Security → Devices.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the app">
    Use the platform's store or the direct download from oxy.so.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Accounts first">
    Accounts is where your Oxy ID lives. Sign in there before any other app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add other apps">
    When you open Inbox or Console, they'll detect your existing session and sign you in automatically via FedCM.
  </Step>
</Steps>

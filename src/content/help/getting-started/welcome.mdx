Welcome. Oxy is an open ecosystem for everyday digital life — your identity, your inbox, your apps. This guide walks you through the pieces and how they fit together.

## What Oxy is

Oxy is a federation of small, focused products that all share one identity. Sign in once with your Oxy ID and every app — Mention, Inbox, Console, Homiio, Allo — remembers you.

<Callout type="info" title="Open by default">
  Every Oxy product is open source. Browse the code on [GitHub](https://github.com/OxyHQ).
</Callout>

## The core ideas

- **One identity** — your Oxy ID is portable and key-based. You own it.
- **End-to-end encrypted** — Inbox messages and Allo chats are encrypted on your device.
- **Federated** — Mention plugs into the wider fediverse so you reach people on Mastodon, Bluesky, and beyond.
- **Self-hostable** — every server can be run by you. We just make the easy path painless.

## Where to go next

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the apps">
    Grab the Oxy apps from the [download page](/technologies) — Accounts, Inbox, Console.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your Oxy ID">
    Open Accounts and follow the recovery-phrase flow. This is your master key.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect what you already use">
    Inbox can pull in existing email accounts so you don't lose history.
  </Step>
</Steps>

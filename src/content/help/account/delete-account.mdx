Account deletion is permanent. We do not keep a "soft-deleted" copy of your data — once the grace period ends, every server-side record tied to your identity is purged.

<Callout type="danger" title="This cannot be undone">
  Deletion immediately revokes every device. After 30 days your data is unrecoverable.
</Callout>

## Before you delete

- **Export anything you want to keep.** Use **Settings → Privacy → Export my data** to download a full archive.
- **Cancel any active subscriptions.** Open the billing screen and end any plans first.
- **Tell your contacts.** End-to-end encrypted Inbox messages stay readable on the other party's device, but new replies will bounce.

## How to delete

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Sign into Accounts and go to **Settings → Privacy**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Delete account">
    Tap the **Delete my account** button at the bottom of the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm with your username">
    Type your full username exactly to confirm. This is intentionally annoying — accidental deletions cost real data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait 30 days or finalize immediately">
    By default we hold your data for 30 days in case you change your mind. Tap **Cancel deletion** during this window to restore everything. After 30 days the purge is final.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What gets deleted

- Your Oxy ID and all device sessions
- Email in Inbox (encrypted blobs and metadata)
- Posts and follows in Mention (federated copies on other servers may persist — this is how the fediverse works)
- Apps and API keys in Console
- All billing records except the legally required minimum (tax records)

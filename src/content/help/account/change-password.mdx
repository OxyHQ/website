You can change your Oxy password from any device, and the change takes effect everywhere instantly — every active session is invalidated and you'll need to sign in again.

## Change it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Security">
    Settings → Security → Password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your current password">
    We ask for your current password before allowing changes. If you can't remember it, use the **Forgot password?** link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a new password">
    Pick something at least 12 characters long. A passphrase ("correct horse battery staple") works better than a short complex one.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

- **Use a password manager.** 1Password, Bitwarden, or your browser's built-in manager — anything is better than reusing a password.
- **Don't reuse this password anywhere else.** If another site is breached, attackers will try the same credentials on Oxy.
- **Turn on two-factor authentication.** A password alone is not enough.

<Callout type="tip">
  Passkeys are a stronger alternative to passwords entirely. Set one up in **Security → Passkeys** if your device supports it.
</Callout>

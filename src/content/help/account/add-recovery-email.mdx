A recovery email is the fastest way to regain access if you lose your device or recovery phrase. We strongly recommend setting one as soon as you create your Oxy ID.

<Callout type="warning" title="Recovery emails are not a substitute for your recovery phrase">
  Your 24-word recovery phrase is the only way to fully restore your identity. The recovery email is a convenience for unlocking sessions — not a master key.
</Callout>

## Add an email

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Accounts">
    Sign in and tap your avatar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Security">
    Choose **Settings → Security** from the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add an email address">
    Tap **Recovery email → Add email**, enter the address, and confirm via the verification link we send.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What it does

When you add a recovery email, Oxy can send you sign-in links and account-change confirmations. We never use it for marketing.

## Change or remove an email

You can change or remove the recovery email at any time from the same screen. Removing your only recovery email leaves your account dependent on the recovery phrase alone — make sure you have it written down somewhere safe before you do.
